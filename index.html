WHAT TO COLLECT:


COMPOST

In the compost game, you should collect the below items.


Apples
Egg Shells

You can also compost old teabags, coffee grinds and the filters, stale cereal, old fruit, and stripes of cardboard. You should wait for at least two weeks for the compost to be ready; it can be used to fertilize the plants in your garden. Don't have a garden? That's okay! You can donate your compost to community gardens, local farms, or a Curbside Compost Service.


RECYCLE

In the recycle game, you should collect the below items.


Aluminum Cans
Newspaper

You can recycle many things like plastic and glass bottles along with aluminum cans and paper. If you are unsure about whether you can recycle it or not, look at the label for a recycling sign that typically has a number within it. If you see this sign, you can recycle it. You can even save five cents by recycling your plastic and glass bottles as well as your aluminum cans in a nearby grocery store!
